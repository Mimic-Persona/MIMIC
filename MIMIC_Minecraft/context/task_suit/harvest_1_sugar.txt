Your final goal is to find material and craft to obtain sugar.
Guidance:
1. Explore a sugar cane plant. They usually grow near water; after you find water, you need to explore for sugar cane.
2. After exploration, dig and collect the sugar cane.
3. Craft a sugar from the sugar cane, and you do not need a crafting table for this.
4. Follow the personality, and once you think you are ready, you have to accomplish the task.
